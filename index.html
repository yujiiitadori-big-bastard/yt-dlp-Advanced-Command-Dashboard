yt-dlp Advanced Download Interface

Full Quality Video + Metadata + Subtitles

  • Best video and audio streams
  • Embeds thumbnail, chapters, metadata
  • English and Hindi subtitles supported
  • Automatic retry and resume
yt-dlp -f "bestvideo+bestaudio/best" -o "%(title)s.%(ext)s" --embed-thumbnail --embed-metadata --embed-chapters --embed-info-json --write-auto-subs --embed-subs --sub-langs "en.*,hi.*" --ignore-errors --retries infinite --fragment-retries infinite --continue
